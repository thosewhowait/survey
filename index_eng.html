User Research: My Neue Pinakothek

Welcome!

Thank you for taking part in this survey.

As a part of Univ.Project, we created new experience model for virtual Curation & Audio guide service.

With this interactive application, you can virtually hold your own exhibition of artworks in the museum “Neue Pinakothek”.

Also, another main function is to record curator’s own audio guide in it’s own mother language, so visitors can listen and

enjoy the audio guide in it’s natural language.

This survey takes maximum 30 minutes and the results of this data will be used anonymously only for this study.

< Task & Process: please try to use the Prototype at the bottom left >

1. Try to select the Curator mode, and search the artwork “Sunflower by van Gogh“ and add this to your exhibition project

2. Next, add the artwork "Young Prasant Woman with three children at the window“ from your collection to your exhibition project.

3. Including Visitor mode, Please feel free to use the Prototype about 10 minutes and answer the question of two surveys.

4. The first survey is Evaluation of attractiveness of the application.so, please tell your impression about App on the survey.

5. Finally, please respond the online interview below.

Again, thank you for helping us with our Inquiry.
